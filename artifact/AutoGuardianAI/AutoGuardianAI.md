# Auto Guardian AI – The Auto Problem Fix

## Introduction
Auto Guardian AI is a conversational AI tool designed to help drivers quickly understand and respond to vehicle diagnostic issues. The system assists users in interpreting vehicle diagnostic codes and translates technical automotive data into simple, actionable guidance. The goal of the project is to reduce the stress and uncertainty drivers experience when warning lights appear by providing clear recommendations and possible next steps.

## Description
Auto Guardian AI is a prototype chatbot developed to assist drivers, DIY mechanics, and small automotive shops in diagnosing vehicle issues. The system allows users to input vehicle information and diagnostic trouble codes, after which the AI analyzes the information and provides explanations, potential causes, and recommended diagnostic actions.

The chatbot is designed to bridge the gap between complex automotive diagnostic data and user understanding. By leveraging a structured knowledge base and conversational AI, the tool transforms technical fault codes into understandable insights that help users make informed decisions about their vehicle repairs. :contentReference[oaicite:0]{index=0}

## Objective
The objective of this artifact was to demonstrate how artificial intelligence can be applied to real-world diagnostic problems. Specifically, the project aimed to:

- Help drivers understand vehicle warning signals and diagnostic codes
- Provide prioritized repair suggestions based on vehicle information
- Reduce unnecessary repair costs caused by misdiagnosis
- Improve decision-making for drivers experiencing vehicle issues

## Process
The project followed the **Design Thinking framework**, which includes the stages of empathize, define, ideate, prototype, and test.

**Empathize:**  
The team identified three primary user groups: everyday drivers, DIY vehicle owners, and small automotive repair shops. Each group needed a fast and reliable way to interpret vehicle diagnostic information.

**Define:**  
The core problem identified was that raw vehicle diagnostic data is often difficult for users to interpret, leading to confusion, incorrect repairs, or unnecessary expenses.

**Ideate:**  
The team designed a three-layer system architecture consisting of:
- A **Data Layer** containing vehicle-specific failure data
- An **AI Layer** that evaluates potential causes and probabilities
- A **User Layer** that translates technical results into plain language

**Prototype:**  
A chatbot prototype was developed using a no-code AI chatbot platform. Users select their vehicle, enter a diagnostic code, and receive recommendations for possible fixes and tests.

**Testing:**  
Multi-user testing was conducted to evaluate the clarity and usefulness of the chatbot responses. Results showed that users trusted the system more when the AI explained why a recommendation was made.

## Tools and Technologies Used
- No-code AI chatbot builder (Mizou platform)
- OBD-II vehicle diagnostic protocols
- Structured knowledge base of vehicle failure patterns
- Conversational AI for user interaction
- Design Thinking methodology

## Value Proposition
This artifact demonstrates how artificial intelligence can simplify complex technical information and make it accessible to everyday users. By transforming vehicle diagnostic codes into understandable recommendations, Auto Guardian AI empowers users to make informed decisions about vehicle maintenance and repairs.

## Unique Value
The unique value of this project lies in its integration of AI-driven reasoning with automotive diagnostics. Instead of simply displaying diagnostic codes, the system interprets the data, prioritizes potential causes, and communicates recommendations in plain language.

Additionally, the system incorporates safety-focused design principles. For example, it avoids providing DIY instructions for critical safety systems such as brakes or steering and instead recommends professional repair services when necessary.
