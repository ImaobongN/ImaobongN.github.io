## Why this timeline matters

Artificial intelligence did not grow in a straight line. Its history includes bold ideas, periods of disappointment, major technical breakthroughs, and a recent explosion in real world impact. This timeline highlights how AI moved from a philosophical question into a technology that now shapes healthcare, finance, education, and everyday productivity.

## What stands out from AI history

### 1. Progress was never linear
AI experienced major setbacks during the two AI winters. These periods showed that strong ideas alone are not enough. Progress also depends on computing power, available data, and realistic expectations.

### 2. Breakthroughs happened when theory met scale
Backpropagation created the learning foundation, but advances like AlexNet only became possible when GPUs and large datasets made deep learning practical.

### 3. AI moved from narrow systems to broad impact
Early systems handled specific tasks like conversation patterns or chess. Today, AI supports diagnosis, fraud detection, content creation, search, and productivity tools.

### 4. The field keeps evolving
The shift from symbolic AI to neural networks, then to transformers and multimodal systems, shows that AI continues to reinvent itself as technology improves.
